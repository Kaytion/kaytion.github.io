Character:
There are four characters you can play as in this game (this character's name is Ironclad). Each character has unique cards only they can get.

They also all have a few unique potions and relics the other characters do not get. They each start off with a deck of cards that are always the same at the start. They also each have slightly different max health.
The characters currently in the game are:
  • The Ironclad
  • The Silent
  • The Defect
  • The Watcher (Steam version only until the console versions get patched)
